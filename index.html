Al Hilal 0-1 Chelsea, Club World Cup

Chelsea came in as the clear favorites, and we got to work right away in living up to that billing. But the first half was a rather familiar story in being unable to take full advantage of promising situations and chances. Fortunately, we did get a lucky bounce off of a defender and Romelu Lukaku made no mistake from point-blank range to give us a slim 1-0 half-time lead. Lukaku had a couple other chances go begging, while Ziyech kept peppering the goalkeeper with shots from distance that lacked the requisite quality to actually result in a goal.
